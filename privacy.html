Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 24, 2026

1. Overview

Allen Enriquez Content Hub ("the App") is a personal content management tool used to schedule and publish videos to TikTok and other social media platforms. This privacy policy explains how the App handles data.

2. Data Collected

The App collects the following data solely to operate its features:

  • TikTok account access tokens (OAuth), stored locally
  • Video metadata (titles, descriptions) for publishing
  • Basic TikTok user profile information (username, user ID) via user.info.basic scope

3. How Data Is Used

Data is used exclusively to authenticate with TikTok and publish content on behalf of the authorised user. No data is sold, shared, or transferred to third parties.

4. Data Storage

Access tokens and metadata are stored in a local SQLite database on the operator's device. No cloud database is used for credential storage.

5. Third-Party Services

The App integrates with the TikTok API. TikTok's own privacy policy governs data processed through their platform.
